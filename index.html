GAME PLAY

  1. At the start of each game, you'll be given a random number.
  2. There are four crystals. Each time you click on one, you add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  3. To win the game, you must match your total score to the random number.
  4. If your total score goes above the random number you'll lose the game.
Losses:

The value of the crystals will be hidden until & while you click them. It's up to you to figure it out!!!

Each time the game restarts, the value of the crystals will change. Again and again and again!!!!
